About Us

Enhancing the quality of life for Deaf individuals in Howard County.

Our Mission

  • Serve and represent the Deaf Community in Howard County.
  • Develop a solid partnership with the government of Howard County.
  • Build relations between hearing and Deaf constituents in Howard County.

Our Resources

We provide a comprehensive list of resources to support the Deaf community, including:

  • Maryland School for the Deaf - K-12 education for the deaf.
  • Gallaudet University - Higher education for the deaf and hard of hearing.
  • National Association of the Deaf - Advocacy and resources at the national level.
  • Maryland Association of the Deaf - State-level advocacy and resources.
  • Bumper Globe Collision Centre & Autowerx - Auto repair and service shop in Baltimore.
  • Dr. Bo A. Byun, DMD, LLC - General dentistry services.

For a full list of resources, visit our Resources Page.
